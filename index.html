Keep the accounts alive

06Ban-avoidance & multi-account (for when you clone)

Phase 1 is one persona, so this is light now — but bake it in from day one so the winner clones cleanly across your stack.

4 isolation layers (all required)

  • IP (Gonzo): one sticky residential IP per persona, geo/tz-matched, never hop countries.
  • Fingerprint (Vantage): one stable distinct profile per persona. Classic death: 20 IPs, one shared device fingerprint = 20 linked accounts.
  • Behavior: stagger posting windows, don't cross-engage your own accounts, don't run the cluster back-to-back in one session. This is what actually links clusters in 2026 — it's on you + a VA.
  • Content de-dup: before a concept hits a 2nd account, change ≥4 of 7 signals (hook, caption, sound, cover, niche, language, time).

Disclosure = cheap insurance

  • Toggle the native AI label on every platform. It satisfies policy and doesn't tank reach.
  • You don't need "AI" plastered on her face — the native toggle is enough.
  • EU AI Act Art. 50 goes live 2 Aug 2026 — labeling becomes law, not optional.
  • Warm new accounts (esp. IG) ~7-14 days of neutral content before any link/offer.
